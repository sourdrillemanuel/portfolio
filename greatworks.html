Integrity & simplicity.
A Great Works redesign concept.

Great Works has become bigger and the website does not reflect the studio anymore. The purpose is to unfold the story about how we creatively solve problems combining magic and logic, display our personality and offer a proper scene for our cases.

We have a strong commitment to minimalism and simplicity. Therefore, we removed all unnecessary items to focus on the content itself.

The objective of the site is to give people a clear understanding of who we are, what do we do and how we (digitally) behave. We need to entice and create interest in our work and our philosophy. We are a small studio of international creatives who genuinely care.
