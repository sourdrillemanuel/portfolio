Bullogram fights breast cancer with traditionnal Swedish "kanelbulle".

Every year in October, Bullogram spreads a important message of prevention and information in order to fight against breast cancer. Using the traditionnal Swedish "kanelbulle" at the center of the campaign, Bullogram gives the opportunity to send tasty cinnamon buns to loved ones throught Facebook. The social media allows a wider distribution.
