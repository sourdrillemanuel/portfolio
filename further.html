Further. Fifty thousand kilometers.

"Further" chronicles the trip of two digital and geography lovers along the Ancient Silk Road. If the main trade route was originally from Istanbul, Turkey to Xi'an, China, they chose to travel from Western France to Beijing by the south, and then from Beijing to France by the north.
