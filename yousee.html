Mærk musikken.
A youSee live event at the Royal Arena.

In October 2017, the youSee Live concert was held in the Royal Arena, in Copenhagen. Four Danish artists played: Rasmus Seebach, Medina, Mads Langer and Tina Dickow. Customers were given the chance to win tickets for this exclusive live experience.

We created a simple one-page layout, with a strong colour presence, giving a dynamic tone to the overall atmosphere. The artists are associated with a quote, a portrait and a video on overlay, all of them set to different speed with a parallax effect. The result is a compelling and energetic digital experience that put the artists and their contributions on the front.
